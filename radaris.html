The Radaris Solution

Radaris is an affordable, radio-signal-based warning system that works independently of weather conditions[cite: 4, 13]. Unlike visual sensors, Radaris uses 2.4 GHz frequencies to communicate between vehicles, ensuring safety even in zero visibility[cite: 30, 47].
